# 🚀 Quick Start - Google Drive Videos

## For Doctors: Add Your First Video in 3 Minutes

### Step 1️⃣: Prepare Your Video (1 minute)
- Have your video file ready (MP4, MOV, etc.)
- Go to [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com)
- Click "New" → "File Upload"
- Select your video
- Wait for upload to finish ⏳

### Step 2️⃣: Share the Video (30 seconds)
1. Right-click the uploaded video
2. Click "Share"
3. Click the dropdown (says "Restricted")
4. Select "**Anyone with the link**"
5. Click "Share"
6. **Copy the link** shown in the dialog

### Step 3️⃣: Add to HorizonFit (1.5 minutes)
1. Go to **Doctor Dashboard**
2. Click **Normal Plan**
3. Click **Videos** tab
4. Click **"Add Video"** button
5. Fill in the form:
   - **Title:** "Zone 1 - Warm Up Routine"
   - **Description:** What the video is about
   - **Video URL:** Paste your Google Drive link here ⬅️ KEY STEP
   - **Duration:** "10:30" (or however long)
   - **Required:** Toggle ON/OFF
6. Click **"Save"**
7. ✅ Done! Video is now live!

## For Patients: Watch Videos

1. Open your **Zone** or **Horizon Guide**
2. Click any **video** to play
3. Watch it 🍿
4. ✅ Automatically marks as complete

## Common Issues

### ❌ "You need access" error
→ Re-share the video with "Anyone with the link"

### ❌ "Invalid URL" message  
→ Make sure you copied the link from the Share dialog, not the browser bar

### ❌ Video won't play
→ Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) and refresh

### ❌ Link works on computer but not phone
→ Refresh the page or try a different browser

## Troubleshoot in 30 Seconds

1. **Copy Google Drive link again**
   - Right-click file → Share → Copy link from dialog
   - Don't use the browser address bar

2. **Check sharing settings**
   - Right-click file → Share
   - Make sure it says "Anyone with the link"
   - If not, change it

3. **Try refreshing**
   - Close tab and reopen
   - Or clear cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)

4. **Still not working?**
   - Try with a YouTube video link instead
   - If that works, the system is OK (issue is with Google Drive sharing)
